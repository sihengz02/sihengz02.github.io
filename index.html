Research

Current topics:

  • Humanoid whole-body control and loco-manipulation: ResMimic (arXiv'25), TWIST2 (arXiv'25), UH-1 (Humanoids'25 Oral).
  • Scaling up robot learning via scalable:
    • data generation from internet images and videos: RoLA (CoRL'25), UH-1 (Humanoids'25 Oral);
    • reward generation: Text2Reward (ICLR'24 Spotlight);
    • simulation and evaluation: RoboVerse (RSS'25 Oral), InternUtopia (arXiv'24).

Previous topics:

  • Language and multimodal agents: OSWorld (NeurIPS'24), Lemur (ICLR'24 Spotlight).
  • Simulation, perception, and manipulation of deformable objects: TieBot (CoRL'24 Oral), DiffClothAI (IROS'23 Oral), ClothesNet (ICCV'23).
