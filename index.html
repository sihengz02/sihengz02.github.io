Siheng Zhao

Greetings! I'm a Senior student at School of Artificial Intelligence, Nanjing University. Nowadays I work as Research Assistant at HKU NLP Group with Prof. Tao Yu.

Previously, I was a funded visiting student at Department of Computer Science, National University of Singapore, where I'm honoured to work with Prof. Lin Shao. I was also an Intern at NJU NLP Group, advised by Prof. Shu-Jian Huang.

My research interests now lie at the intersection of Natural Language Processing and Reinforcement Learning. My long-term goal is to develop a language-centric agent that can interact with the physical world (Robot Agent), browse websites (Web Agent), play video games (Game Agent) and process data (Data Agent).

Previously I mainly focused on Robot Learning, especially high-dimensional deformable cloth simulation, manipulation, and perception.
