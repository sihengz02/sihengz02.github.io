Research

My research interests now lie at the intersection of Reinforcement Learning and Natural Language Processing. My long-term goal is to build the next generation of autonomous and intelligent agents that can proactively sense, plan, and interact with both the physical and digital world.

To achieve this goal, I primarily focus on the following topics:

  • Developing efficient reasoning and learning algorithms for language-based agents.
  • Advancing language-based agents for multimodal, unstructured and 3D-aware environments.
  • Building robust agent benchmarks with executable environments and human-centric metrics.

Specifically, I'm interested in the following areas: Embodied AI, Large Langauge Models & Vision-language Models, Code Generation.
